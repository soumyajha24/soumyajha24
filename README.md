## 🧠 About Me

I'm **Soumya Kumari**, an aspiring **AI/ML Engineer** and **Data Scientist** from India 🇮🇳 — passionate about building intelligent systems that turn raw data into real-world impact.

I speak **Python** fluently and teach machines to think 🤖 My work spans across **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **Computer Vision**, and **Data Science** — always with the goal of solving problems that actually matter.

- 🔭 &nbsp;Currently diving deep into **Generative AI & LLMs**
- 🌱 &nbsp;Always learning — right now exploring **Prompt Engineering**
- 💡 &nbsp;I believe data has the power to change the world
- 🎯 &nbsp;Goal: Build AI that creates **meaningful, measurable impact**
- 🤝 &nbsp;Open to **collaborations**, **research**, and **opportunities**
